About TaskConnect

TaskConnect is a trusted platform that bridges the gap between customers and skilled workers. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, carpenter, or cleaner, TaskConnect makes it simple to find and book reliable services.

Our Mission

To empower workers by providing them with opportunities and to give customers easy access to skilled professionals they can trust.

Our Vision

To become India’s most reliable service platform where customers can book trusted professionals anytime, anywhere — and workers can grow their careers with dignity.

Our Values

  • Trust & Transparency
  • Quality Service
  • Customer Satisfaction
  • Worker Empowerment
